💡 Zero-Waste Tips
🥕 Use vegetable peels
Carrot and potato peels can be roasted with oil and salt for a crispy, nutritious snack.
🧅 Regrow spring onions
Place onion roots in a glass of water on a sunny windowsill. They'll regrow in a few days!
🍞 Stale bread ideas
Stale bread makes great breadcrumbs, croutons, French toast, or a panzanella salad.
